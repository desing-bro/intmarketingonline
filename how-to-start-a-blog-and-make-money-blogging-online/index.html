How To Start A Blog And Make Money Blogging Online

October 12, 2016 Olivia Nkwarulo

Anyone can Make Money blogging by starting a blog, but there are a few things that will separate those that earn a bit of extra cash on the side as a hobby and the full-time professional bloggers.

In this article we will quickly go over the best way for how to start a blog in order to Make Money Blogging, as well as reveal one of the most lucrative ways to fast-track your online success.

1. Pick A Niche – Before you start a blog you need to have a good understanding of your target audience and thus the niche you will be a part of.

The subject area of your blog needs to be very specific and targeted. It is much harder to make Money Blogging with very generic content and visitors.

It is best to blog about a subject area that you are interested in, but as long as it is in a lucrative niche. It will be hard going blogging about some obscure niche that only you and a handful of other people share an interest in!

2. Build A Relationship – Anyone can start a blog, but the people who actually make money by blogging are those that build a relationship with their followers.

Also, when you are adding excellent content to your blog and are building a relationship then it takes the pressure off you from needing to be good at SEO and getting high Google rankings. Because you will get traffic simply from people sharing your content with others because of its quality.

So focus on entertaining and giving value to your visitors and being active on social media platforms to building your following as opposed to trying to become a technical SEO expert.

3. High Paying Affiliate Programs – The final step to making money from your blog is to sell products to your visitors. The easiest way to start doing this is with affiliate marketing, by promoting existing products for commissions.

Many bloggers seem to do well with building a big following via their content, but then fail miserably when it comes to making money from their blog.

So to make sure that you do not fall into the same trap, make sure that you are not afraid to ask for the sale. If you are promoting quality affiliate products that give genuine value to the customer then you are providing an excellent service and getting paid the money that you deserve.

You are no good to your blog followers if you are not earning enough to do it full-time and thus go out of business!
